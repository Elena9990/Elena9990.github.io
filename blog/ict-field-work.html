ICT field of work

Facebook

Facebook, now part of Meta, focuses on connecting people globally through social media, online advertising, and emerging virtual platforms like the metaverse. It primarily uses technologies such as AI, machine learning, data analytics, and cloud computing to power its services. With over 60,000 employees worldwide, Facebook operates as a multinational company with offices in more than 80 cities. Its culture emphasizes speed, innovation, and openness, encouraging employees to solve problems creatively and make quick decisions. Facebook offers a wide range of roles, including software engineering, data science, product management, and cybersecurity.

Facebook has been a big part of my life, because I've had a profile there since I was a child and I've been so curious how everything in the app is just made. For instance, the fact that you can find so many poeple and see everything about them is quite interesting to see how it is done. The other reason that I would like to work in the company would be that it could give me a lot of marketing and coding experience. Another opportunity that I would add is that I can improve the system as well and be a part of that large team. Also, I'd like to see how all the information about all the users has been used and where exactly it goes.

YouTube

YouTube is a video-sharing platform owned by Google that works on projects related to content creation, streaming, and video recommendation algorithms. The platform heavily utilizes cloud services, AI for content recommendations, and large-scale data processing technologies. YouTube operates internationally, boasting over 2 billion monthly users, and it is a key player in the digital entertainment and tech industries. YouTube's culture encourages collaboration, creativity, and innovation. Employees work in roles such as software engineering, product management, and content policy.

There are several reasons because of which I would like to work for YouTube. Firtly, it's been improving all the time and I as a user can say that it's always been intriguing to see the changes and actually think of a way to make some on my own. I would like to try to improve the desgin and to add more options to the platform. Also, I'd like to see the whole process "behind the scenes", for example, how they make the mecahcnism of approving the videos and how the regulations are being implemented.

Spotify

Spotify is a leading music streaming platform that works on projects related to audio streaming, podcasting, and personalized recommendations. It relies on AI, machine learning, and cloud technologies to deliver personalized music experiences to users. Spotify has over 9,000 employees and operates globally, with headquarters in Sweden and offices in various cities worldwide. The company fosters a flexible, inclusive, and innovation-driven culture, with job roles ranging from software engineering and data science to music curation and marketin​.

Joining Spotify as an IT specialist means working at the forefront of digital music technology, with access to cutting-edge tools and a culture of innovation. You’ll tackle complex challenges, collaborate with top talent, and have a direct impact on enhancing user experience for millions worldwide. Spotify's commitment to growth and technological excellence makes it an exciting place to advance your career and contribute to groundbreaking projects.
